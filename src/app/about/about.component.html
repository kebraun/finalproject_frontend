Our Vision

Every year, approximately 3.2 million new cats enter our shelter-system. With the emergence of COVID-19, shelters that were already competing for funding and supplies are struggling to maintain basic operations. Now, more than ever, shelters are looking for potential adopters.

To give the shelters a hand, we came up with a website to help bridge the gap between adopter and adoptee. Using data provided by theCatAPI, we pair you with your perfect breed based on your ideal cat. Do you prefer a quiet cat, or a talkative cat? A lazy cat, or a cat that likes to be played with? Then, using the Petfinder API, we supply you with a list of adoptable cats based on the breeds you were matched with!

*This website is built with Angular, NodeJS, and Express.
